The Albany City Council
[ a roster of officeholders, 1686-1800 ]

Listed below are the officers of the Albany Corporation beginning in 1686. This roster has been compiled from Common Council Minutes and other sources. The annual rosters shown below cover the calendar year from September and/or October (times of election and appointment) to the same month of the year following.

notes

*All municipal officers were elected and appointed for one year at a time. Typically, elections took place late in September; officers were sworn in in October; officers served thru the new year until superceded or re-appointed the following September/October.

Common Council minutes also known as "The City Records" were transcribed and printed by Joel Munsell and are the source for this roster. Mostly, the transcribed volumes are available online.
