notes

List transformed from New York Colonial Documents ("a M.S. in the office of the Secretary of Stte in Albany"). Printed in Annals, vol. 2, pp 186-88. That version includes Rensselaerswyck but not the other jurisdictions in Albany County. These have been transcribed and appear online. Freeholders' lists are discussed generally in the CAP Guide.

William Hogan (the elder) filed a will that passed probate in 1739 - meaning that he was deceased!
