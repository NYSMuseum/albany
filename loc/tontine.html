"The Tontine Coffee House"

Still without substantial defining information, in 2017 we felt the need to move forward with a webpage devoted to this legendary Albany location. At this point, what follows is an admittedly subjective compilation of existing materials from Albany's most revered traditional sources. No ownership of anything that follows is claimed as this offering is simply FYI! Stefan Bielinski.

Random Recollections



notes

This description page relies on community-based resources but also incorporates traditional sources and lore but with cautions where appropriate. Its purpose is to introduce new information and perspective on an element of the "what" and "where" in the lives of the people of colonial Albany and not to embellish the existing material/wisdom/lore. It does, however, it provide some links to the most useful of the existing sources.
