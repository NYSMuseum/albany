Albany Population Statistics


Listed below in chronological order are enumerated and estimated population figures for the city of Albany. These statistics are not based on the research actually conducted by the Colonial Albany Social History Project. Instead, each statistic represents what we will call the standard sources (aka what passes for so-called "conventional wisdom") either identified by following the links on each statistic /and/or date or are taken from official US Census data. They provide a general simple sense of the parameters of community demographics. As is true with all statistics, none of them is perfect!
