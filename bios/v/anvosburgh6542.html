Anna Vosburgh Vanderwerken Vandenbergh

by
Stefan Bielinski


Anna Vosburgh was born in November 1725. She was the daughter of Abraham and Geertje Vandenbergh Vosburgh.

Her first husband appears to have been Roeloff Vanderwerken, Jr. The first of their six children was chirstened in the Albany Dutch church in January 1746. By 1757, five more children had been baptized in Albany In November 1761, the widow would have been about thirty-six when she married Willem Vandenbergh. Perhaps two more children were born during the 1760s.

This profile of Anna Vosburgh Vanderwerken Vandenbergh is admittedly pre-mature.
