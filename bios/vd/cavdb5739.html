Catharina Vandenbergh Marselis
by
Stefan Bielinski


Catharina Vandenbergh was born during the 1620s or 30s.

By 1655, she had married Beverwyck pioneer Hendrick Marselis and was raising a family.

These Marselises lived in the Albany County countryside and also in Albany.

Catharina Vandenbergh Marselis was dead by 1697. Her husband died later that year.
