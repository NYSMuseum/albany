Elizabeth Van Dyck Vosburgh

by
Stefan Bielinski


Elizabeth Van Dyck Vosburgh was born in 1692. She was the daughter of Hendrick and Maria Schuyler Van Dyck. She was one of four children living in the Van Dyck home in Albany in 1697. Her father was a prominent surgeon who drowned in 1707.

At some time after the doctor's death, widow Maria relocated her family to Kinderhook.

In September 1725, Elizabeth married widower Martin Vosburgh of Kinderhook. The marriage is said to have produced a daughter in 1729. Marriage and baptism information appears absent from the available records of the Albany Dutch church.
