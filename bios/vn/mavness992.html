Maycke Van Ness Wendell

by
Stefan Bielinski


Maycke Van Ness was born during the 1670s. She probably was the daughter of one branch of the Van Ness family.

In April 1698, she married Abraham Wendell. By 1716, eight or nine children had been christened at the Albany Dutch church where she was a member.

These Wendells lived in Albany's first ward but were rather insonspicuous Albany residents.

Abraham Wendell died in 1755. Maycke Van Ness Wendell was last heard from in 1716.
