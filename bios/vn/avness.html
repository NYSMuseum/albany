"A." Van Ness Fonda

by
Stefan Bielinski


In October 1732, "A. Van Ness" married "D. Fonda at the Dutch church in Albany. A marriage license had been submitted. The groom was Albany or Albany-area native Douw J. Fonda. The first name of the subject of this sketch is less certain.

Based on deduction and elimination, the bride, "A. Van Ness " probably was born within a few years of 1710. Her first name probably was a version of Alida or Anna. We seek to connect her to a Van Ness family of Albany or its hinterland.

Between 1733 and 1744, the marriage is known to have produced five children. However, another/different couple Douw and Alida Fonda also christened children before and following this subject's date of marriage.

"A's" husband was an Albany resident whose named appeared in the community-based record at least until 1743. We seek information on his later life and passing.

With large defining issues still unresolved regarding the life of A. Van Ness Fonda, we move on for now.




notes

Sources: The life of A. Van Ness Fonda has no CAP biography number. This sketch is derived chiefly from family and community-based resources.
