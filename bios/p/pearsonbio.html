Jonathan Pearson
by
Stefan Bielinski


Jonathan Pearson was born in Chichester, New Hampshire in 1813. He was descended from the Puritan settlers of Massachusetts. En route to a better future in Ohio, his father, Caleb Pearson, was so impressed by Union College that he settled the family in Schenectady. Jonathan graduated from Union College in 1835.

After serving as a tutor, he became professor of Mathematics, Chemistry, and Botany at Union. He later served as College Librarian and Treasurer into the 1870s.

Jonathan Pearson died in 1887.
