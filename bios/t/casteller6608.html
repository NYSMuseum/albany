Caspar Teller

by
Stefan Bielinski


Caspar (Casparus) Teller was the son of New Netherland pioneer Willem Teller. He probably was born during the middle decades of the seventeenth century.

By the 1690s, he was living in New York where he witnessed baptisms in 1692 and 1695.

We seek additional information on his life and passing!




notes

Sources: The life of Caspar Teller is CAP biography number 6608. This sketch is derived chiefly from family and community-based resources. At this point, his existence only raises questions.
