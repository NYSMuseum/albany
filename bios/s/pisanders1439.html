Pieter Sanders

by
Stefan Bielinski


Pieter Sanders was born in November or December 1755. He was the son of Albany leader Robert Sanders and his second wife, a much younger Elizabeth Schuyler Sanders. Pieter grew up in a large, combined, elite family and lived in several conspicuous locations.

Pieter Sanders was not yet ten alive when his father filed a will in 1765. He was named as the eldest and only son. He was scheduled to receive substantial lands and properties.

However, Pieter probably did not live long enough to marry. His father is said to have left no male successors!




notes

Sources: The life of Pieter Sanders is CAP biography number 1439. This sketch is derived chiefly from family and community-based resources. We seek defining information on his passing.
