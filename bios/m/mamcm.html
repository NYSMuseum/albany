Mary Mc Millen Campbell

by
Stefan Bielinski


Referenced variously, Mary Mc Millen/Mullen probably was born during the 1750s.

In July 1778, she married Alexander Campbell at the Albany Dutch church. By 1781, two children had been christened there. Between 1787 and 1791, three more children were christened at the Albany Presbyterian church where both parents would be longterm members.

These Campbells were Albany residents during the 1780s and 90s and possibly earlier. Church records identified him as a laborer. In 1790, their first ward household included two adults and four boys.

After the 1790s, their name is absent from the community-based record.

We seek information on the later life and passing of Mary Mc Millen Campbell




notes

Sources: The life of Mary Mc Millen Campbell has not been assigned a CAP biography number. This sketch is derived chiefly from family and community-based resources.
