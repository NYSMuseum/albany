Susanna Lansing Nack
by
Stefan Bielinski


Susanna Lansing probably was born during the 1660s or 70s. She was the daughter of Albany baker Gerrit and Elsje Van Den Uythoff Lansing.

She joined the Albany Dutch church in 1683. In July 1698, she married Albany resident Matthys Nack at her church. Their twin sons were christened there in February 1699.

However, her marriage was short-lived. Susanna Lansing Nack was dead by December 1702 when Matthys Nack re-married.



notes

Sources: The life of Susanna Lansing Nack is CAP biography number 3684. This sketch is derived chiefly from family and community-based resources.
