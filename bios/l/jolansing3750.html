Johannes G. Lansing

by
Stefan Bielinski


Johannes G. Lansing was born in August 1726. He was the son of Gerrit and Engeltie Van Deusen Lansing.

In October 1761, he married Catharina Burhans at the Albany Dutch church. By 1783, nine children had been christened in Albany. Catharina Burhans died in October 1799 at the age of sixty-one and was buried by her son, Myndert Lansing - a prominent Albany resident of that time .

In June 1775, the city records mentioned Johannes G. Lansing as being a deceased member and the brother of Gerrit Lansing, Jr.

At this point, we hesitate to move forward with this biography! Substantial community based information is available.
