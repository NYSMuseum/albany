Susanna Lansing Hogan
by
Stefan Bielinski


Susanna Lansing was born in November 1715. She was the daughter of Albany mainstays Isaac and Jannetje Beeckman Lansing.

In January 1740, she married blacksmith William Hogan, the younger.. By 1759, their eight children were baptized at the Albany Dutch church.

These Hogans lived modestly near and like their parents in Albany's first ward.

Susanna Lansing Hogan died sometime after February 1778 when she witnessed a baptism with her husband. She was not mentioned in her husband's will filed in 1787.



notes

Sources: The life of Susanna Lansing Hogan is CAP biography number 3682. This profile is derived chiefly from family and community-based resources.
