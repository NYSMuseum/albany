Samuel Bromley

by
Stefan Bielinski

Samuel Bromley was christened in the Albany Dutch church on August 4, 1751. He was the son of marginal mainstays William and Lena Boom Bromley. He probably was named for his mother's brother who witnessed his baptism.

However, the child of "Billie" Bromley was buried from the church later in August 1751. A second Samuel Bromley was christened by those parents (same sponsors) in October of 1753 and lived a long Albany life. Thus, this Samuel probably died shortly after his birth.




notes

Sources: The life of Samuel Bromley is CAP biography number 5962. This sketch is derived chiefly from family and community-based resources.
