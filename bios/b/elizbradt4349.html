Elizabeth Bradt Visscher
by
Stefan Bielinski


Elizabeth Bradt Visscher was born in October 1740. She was the first child born to Hendrick and Rebecca Van Vechten Bradt. Her father died before she reached her sixth birthday.

In May 1763, she married Johannes Visscher. By 1782, eight of their children were baptized at the Albany Dutch church where she was an occasional baptism sponsor.

Elizabeth Bradt Visscher died after 1782. Her husband lived until 1821!

Sources: The life of Elizabeth Bradt Visscher is CAP biography number 4349. This profile is derived chiefly from family and community-based resources.
