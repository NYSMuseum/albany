Wyntie Bogardus Bradt

by
Stefan Bielinski


Wyntie Bogardus was born in September 1710. She was the daughter of Anthony and the widow Jannetie Knickerbacker Lansing Bogardus. She grew up in a woodworker's home in Albany's first ward.

In April 1732, she married Daniel A. Bradt at the Albany Dutch church. By 1735, three children had been christened in Albany where she was an occasional baptism sponsor.

We seek information on the later life and passing of Wyntie Bogardus Bradt




notes

Sources: The life of Wyntie Bogardus Bradt is CAP biography number 6391. This sketch is derived chiefly from family and community-based resources.
