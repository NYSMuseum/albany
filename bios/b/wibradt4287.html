Willempie Bradt

by
Stefan Bielinski


Willempie Bradt was born in May 1735. She was the daughter of Daniel and Wyntie Bogardus Bradt.

In May 1756, she married Daniel B. Bradt at the Albany Dutch church. By 1772, six children had been christened at the Albany church.

These Bradts first lived in the first ward where Daniel worked with his father operating the ferry. Later, they may have re-located to the second ward.

At some point, Daniel Bradt moved out to eastern Rensselaer County. He died at Hoosic in 1805. We seek information on Willempie Bradt Bradt's later life and passing.




notes

Sources: The life of Willempie Bradt is CAP biography number 4287. This sketch is derived chiefly from family and community-based resources.
