Jellis Douwse Fonda

by
Stefan Bielinski

Spelled and referenced to variously, Jellis Douwse Fonda probably was the first member of his family to emigrate to New Netherland. Some family-based resources state that he was born in Amsterdam, Holland in 1604 and was the son of Douwe Fonda. A birth date of 1615 in Friesland also appears in traditional literature However, we seek definitive information on his origins.

His wife is said to have been one Hillegont Claeswe a native of Bommel.
