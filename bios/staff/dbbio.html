Juandrea Bates


Drea Bates was a student intern majoring in History at the State University College at Oneonta.

During the Spring and Summer of 2004, she worked at the Project helping create an Internet version of Anne Grant's classic Memoirs of an American Lady

In 2005, she was named "History Scholar" by the Gilder Lehrman Institute of American History.

During the time we knew her, she became ill and was not able to complete her internship.

However, she seems to have graduated from SUNY Oneonta and to have flourished as a historian.

In fact, all these links seem to be related to her development as a historian!
