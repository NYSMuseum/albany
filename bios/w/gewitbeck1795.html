Geertruy Witbeck Van Vechten
by
Stefan Bielinski


Geertruy Witbeck was born in 1692. She was the daughter of Lucas J. and Catharina Van Deusen Witbeck.

She married Reuben Van Vechten in May 1718. Seven months later, the first of her eight children was baptized in the Albany Dutch church.

These Van Vechtens lived in Albany's third ward.

Her husband died prematurely in 1735 leaving Geertruy to raise her large family with the support of the Witbecks. Geertruy Witbeck Van Vechten died in November 1745 and was buried in the Patroon's vault.



notes

The life of Geertruy Witbeck Van Vechten is CAP biography number 1795. This profile is derived chiefly from family and community-based resources.
