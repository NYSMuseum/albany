Mary Walker Tuttle

by
Stefan Bielinski

According to family-based information, one-time Albany resident Mary Walker was born near the year 1750. She probably was the daughter of Peter and Hannah Fuller Walker of Windham County, Connecticut. We seek defining information on her precise origins and path to Albany.

She was the wife of one-time Albany newcomer David Tuttle. The couple had at least six children.

She was accounted for in the Tuttle household in 1800 in the first ward.

Mary Walker Tuttle died in April 1802. Her stone reads that she died at age 46 and that she was the wife of David Tuttle.




notes

Sources: The life of Mary Walker Tuttle has no CAP biography number. This sketch is derived chiefly from family and community-based resources. The basic Walker family genealogy contains references to her that do not appear to fit this profile.
