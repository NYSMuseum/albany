Robert Wright

by
Stefan Bielinski


Robert Wright was identified as a merchant in Albany in 1781.

The information that follows is intended to develop our profile of this Albany resident. Thus, it is deliberately cautious.

Perhaps he was born in Pennsylvania in 1741.

Perhaps this individual also was Capt. Robert Wright of the First Canadian Regiment (James Livingston). This individual was sent to the upper Hudson Valley to recruit soldiers to serve in Livingston's regiment. He was given a 12-month leave from the regiment in June 1779 and did not return.

Robert Wright seems to have made a faint impression on the Albany community landscape. We must move on from his profile until we have more defining information.




notes

Sources: The life of Robert Wright has not been assigned a CAP biography number. This sketch is derived chiefly from family and community-based resources.
