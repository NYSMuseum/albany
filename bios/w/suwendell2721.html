Susanna Wendell Van Schaick

by
Stefan Bielinski


Susanna Wendell was born about 1685. Although we expect that she was a daughter of the Albany Wendell family, we have yet to definitively match her to specific parents. She is said to have been the daughter of Johannes Wendell. At least seven other Susanna Wendells are at risk during her effective lifetime.

In December 1707, she married Anthony Van Schaick at her parents' house and under the auspices of the Albany Dutch church. Their marriage appears to have been childless as she was dead by May 1712 when Van Schaick re-married.

We seek more defining information on the short life of Albany native Susanna Wendell Van Schaick




notes

Sources: The life of Susanna Wendell Van Schaick is CAP biography number 2721. This sketch is derived chiefly from family and community-based resources.
