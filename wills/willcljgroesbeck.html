Will of Claes J. Groesbeck - 1707



Claes Jacobs Groesbeck, of Albany City. Wife Elizabeth, children Jacob, William, Barber, wife of Gysbert Marcelis, Rebecca, wife of Dominicus van Schaak, Johannis and Stephanus[;] children of dec’d. da. Catrine, late wife of Jacob Teunissen. Real and Personal estate. The wife sole executrix.

Witnesses Willem Jacobsen, Claes Vonda and Robert Livingston.



notes

Will January 3, 1707. Probated March 20, 1713. Printed in Calendar of Wills, #750.
