Will of Isaac Hogan - 1773



In the name of God, Amen. January 5, 1773. I, Isaac Hogan, of Albany, blacksmith, being very sick. I leave to my wife Mary all my estate, real and personal, and make her executor.

Witnesses, Jelles De Garmo, shoemaker, Herman Hun, Jacob De Garmo.

Proved, November 18, 1774.



notes

Will dated January 3, 1773. Probated November 18, 1774. Printed in Abstract of Wills volume 32, p. 290.
