Estate of Gabriel Thompson (Stridles) - 1702

Inventory of the estate of Gabriel Thompson, alias Stridells, of New York. Taken by Jacobus D’ Honeur and Jan Jansen, October 19, 1702.

House and ground, House not finished, £280.
Silver Tankard, 24 ounces, at 7s, 6d, £12, 15s.
2 full pipes of Maerira wine, £50.
100 gallons Madera wine, £20.
40 gallons of Brandy at 12s, £24.
5 gallons of Rum, £1, 5s.

Total amount, £604.

Exhibited as a true account of estate of Gabriel Thompson, deceased, by Isaac [should be Jane Van Laer], April 3, 1703.



notes

Dated October 19, 1702. Printed in Abstract of Wills volume 25, pp. 311.
