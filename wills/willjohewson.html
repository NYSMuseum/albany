Will of John Hewson - 1777



HEWSON, John, of Albany City, cordwainer.

Wife Annatie, sons Daniel and Casparus. Real and personal estate.

Executors bro. Daniel Hewson and Rutger Bleecker, both of Albany.

Witnesses Harms. A. Wendell, Jacob A. Wendell and Rutger Bleecker.




notes

Will dated December 4, 1777. Probated September 15, 1799. Printed in Calendar of Wills, item #848. We seek a more complete transcription of the document. It does not appear to be included in the AW series.
