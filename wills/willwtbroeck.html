Will of Wessel Ten Broeck - 1723



In the name of God, Amen. Wessel Ten Broeck and wife Catharine, da. and heiress of Jacob Lokermans, of Albany City.

Sons Dirck, Jacob, Cornelis, daughters Anna Catharyna, wife of Anthony van Schaick, Cristyna, wife of David van Dyck.

Houses and lots on Brewers Street, Albany; farms in Lokers, West side of Hudson River; on Wanton Island Kill; on Catrix Kill; on Bever Kill (Green Co.); personal estate.

Executors the survivor with the three sons. Witnesses John Myngall, Johs. Vinhaghen, and Arent Pruyn.



notes

Will dated June 6, 1723. Probated January 29, 1752. Printed in Calendar of Wills, #1775. Original manuscript copy resides in "Albany Co. Records, Wills," 1:215.
