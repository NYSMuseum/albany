Will of Archibald Campbell - 1793

Archibald Campbell surveyor.

Wife Christina. Sons Jacob, John, and four others, four daughters.

Lands in New Jersey and near Sacandaga.

House and lot in Albany; personal property.


notes

Will dated February 19, 1793. Probated June 6, 1793. Albany County Surrogate Court, (IW p. 28, only information given).
