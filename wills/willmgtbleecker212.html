Will of Margaret Collins - 1760



In the name of God, Amen. I, MARGARET COLLINS, of Albany, widow of Edward Collins, being sick.

I leave to Marte Gerrittse Van Bergen my negro man " Dick." To Angentie Scott, widow, and her sister, Elizabeth Williams, each £20. To my 2 brothers, John Rutse Bleecker and Johanes Bleecker, all the rest of my estate, real and personal. I make my brother, John R. Bleecker, executor.

Dated April 28, 1760.

Witnesses, Barent H. Ten Eyck, Staats Van Santvoord, Catlyna Van Ness.

Proved before John De Peyster, Esq., June 11, 1760.
