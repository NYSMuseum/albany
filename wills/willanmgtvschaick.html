Will of Anna Maragrita Van Schaick - 1761


[Calendar entry dated] 1761 March 9

van SCHAICK, Anna Margreta, da. of Anthony of Albany City.

Sisters Catrena Coeman and Gerretje ten Eyck, Goose van Schaick jun., son of bro. Goose, daughters of bro. Anthony van Schaick, namely Maria, Anna, wife of Henry ten Broeck and Christina, wife of Anthony van Schaick jun., son of bro. Goose, Tobias, son of Coenraet ten Eyck, Anna Margreta ten Eyck.

Real and personal estate.

Executors sisters Gerretie ten Eyck and Catriena Coeman, with Anthony van Schaick jun.

Witnesses Isaac Staats, Willem Staats, yeomen, and Abrm. Yates jun.

"Albany Co. Records, Wills, I., p. 286."



notes

Will dated March 9, 1761. Probated December 8. 1763. Printed in Calendar of Wills, #2000 on p. 452. Not found in volume 30 of AW (12/13).
