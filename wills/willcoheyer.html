Will of Conradt Kyer (Heyer) - 1760


KYER, Coenradt, of Albany City, carpenter [from CW]

Albany, June the 7, 1760. In the name of God, Amen. This being my last will, I do empower my loving friend, Adam Ramser, to receive my whole dues that is due me in the King's works, and he is to pay all my debts, and the other money he is to hold for his own property.

This being my last will. [from AW]

[signed] "Conerad Hyer."

Witnesses, Samuel La Rue, doctor, & Henry Cole, both of Rensselaer Manor, and Wilhelm Otto.

Proved, June 11, 1760, and [letters of administration] granted to Adam Ramser



notes

Will dated June 7, 1760. Probated June 11, 1760. Printed in Calendar of Wills, #976 on p. 223 and in AW vol. 30, p 18. Some editorial adjustments needed to sensibly reconcile material from two sources.
