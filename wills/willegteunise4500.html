Will of Egbert Teunissen - 1704

[written in Dutch] "In den Namen Goddes, Amen," December 2, 1704. I, Egbert Teunisse, of the city of Albany. Leaves property to my children Teunis, Dirck, Susanah, and wife Margaret [sic].

[The Calendar of Wills entry also notes "Real and personal property (a bible, a silver cup)."]



notes

 Will dated December 2, 1704. Witnesses, Hendrick Hansen, Johannes Pruyn, Wouter Quackenbos. Probated June 26, 1716. Printed in Abstract of Wills volume 26, p. 171; and in Calendar of Wills, entry #1688.
