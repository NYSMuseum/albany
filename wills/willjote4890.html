Will of John H. Ten Eyck - 1792


John H. Ten Eyck, Albany

Heirs:

wife Sarah
neph.: Hendrick, Harmanus - s/o his bro Jacob
neices: Annatie, Margaret - d/o his bro Jacob
neph.: Myndert, Hendrick, John D.P., Jacob, Barent - s/o his bro. Tobias
neph.: John Bleecker, s/o his sis Margaret
neice: Helena Schuyler, d/o his bro Barent, w/o Stephen Schuyler
Hendrick, g/s of Barent Ten Eyck
Annatie, w/o Dr. Mancius

exec: wife Sarah, b/l Abraham Ten Broeck

notes

Will dated May 24, 1792. Probated December 20, 1794. Printed as written in Abstracts of the Wills, p. 132.
