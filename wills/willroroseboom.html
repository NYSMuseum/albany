Will of Robert Roseboom - 1746



ROSE BOOM, Robert, of Albany City, merchant. Aug. 8 Wife Ryckje, brothers Hendrick, Ahasverus, Geysbert and John, sister Elsje Roseboom.

Real and personal property.

The wife sole executrix.

Witnesses Robt. Sanders, merchant, William Rogers, and Abraham Wendell.




notes

Will dated August 8, 1746. Probated April 9. 1764. Printed in Calendar of Wills, number 1471. Does not appear in the Abstracts of Wills series.

A number of Abraham Wendells were alive at that time.
