Will of Gerritie Ten Eyck - 1756


ten EYCK, Gerritie, widow of Koenradt, da. of Anthony van Schaick, of Albany City.

Children Jacob C., Anthony, Barent, Tobias, Andries, Maragrieta, Gerritie, wife of Peter Gansevoort, grandda. Elisabeth, da. of Gerrit Bratt dec,d, heirs and executors of real and personal estate



notes

 Will dated November 19, 1756. Probated October 13, 1768. Printed in Calendar of Wills, #1776. No abstract found in AW.
