Will of - Johannes Roseboom 1737



Roseboom, Johannis. Albany. Will;

Bequeaths property in Albany, Schenectady and along the Mohawk;

Names as heirs wife Gerritie; sons Henderyck, Gerrit, Johannis; daughters Gertruy, Margariet Bleecker, Anna Van Schayck;

Jurian Hoogen, Derrick Wesselse, Benjamin Egbartz, Glocham Lansing, Johannis E. Wendell, Nicholas Bleecker jr. (son in law).

D.S. November 14, 1737. 3p. 12.75 x 8 in. In Dutch.



notes

Will described in the John N. Bleecker Papers, New York State Library; box 1, item 13. Box 1 also includes items related to his real estate and a statement of his accounts, 1765-67 (item 23) by/for his heirs.

Not found in Calendar of Wills or Abstracts of Wills.
