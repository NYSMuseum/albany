Will of Peter Van Olinda - 1715



Van Olinda, Peter, of Rensselaerswyck Manor, tailor.

Sons Daniel, Jacob, Mathys. Land above Schinectade on Mohawk R., do. in the Boght of the Kahoos, personal property.

Executors Col. Peter Schuyler, Col. Kiliaen van Rensselaer and Mr. Johannis Cuyler.

Witnesses Abrm. Cuyler, Justice, Dirk ten Broeck and Hend. Cuyler jun.

Albany Co. Records, Wills, I., p. 148.



notes

Will dated August 1, 1715. Probated December 27, 1716. Printed in Calendar of Wills, p. 449.
