Will of George Archer - 1812



ARCHER, George, of Albany. Wife Mary, sons John, Edward, William, James, George and Michael, daughter Jane.

House, store and lots on Pine Street, corner Eagle, Albany,

yellow store and three lots on Eagle Street, binding on Lutheran,
lot corner Eagle and Steuben Streets.

Executors the wife and John Ervin.

Witnesses Ebenezer Smith, Christopher Monk, David Johnson.



notes

Will dated May 24, 1812 . Probated October 21, 1823. Printed in Calendar of Wills, #42.

Transformed by SB as printed online from CW, #42.
