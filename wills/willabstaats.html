Will of Abraham Staats - 1683


In the name of God, Amen. Appeared before me, Robert Livingston, of Albany, on the 21 day of April, 1683, MAJOR ABRAHAM STAATS, who leaves his estate to his wife, Tryntie Joachims, during her life, and then to his children, Sarah, Isaac, Joachim, Samuel, Elizabeth and Abraham Staats, Jr., and to Bruyne, son of Catharine Staats, deceased.

Witnesses, Adrian Gerittse Popendorf, Peter Maser, Peter Maser Vrooman.

Proved, October 23, 1694.
