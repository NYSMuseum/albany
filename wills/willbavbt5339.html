Will of Baltus Van Benthuysen - 1720



van BENTHUYSEN, Baltus, of Albany City, merchant. Wife Liedia, children Johannis, Catrena, Jacobus Perreker, and Elizabeth.

House and lot in Albany City, do. in Kingston, Ulster Co., personal estate.

The wife sole executrix, brothers Marten and Barent van Benthuysen and Mr. Barent Sanders tutors and trustees.

Witnesses Barent Sanders, Justice, Augustinus Turck and Marten van Benthuysen.



notes

Will dated November 5, 1720. Probated March 31, 1725. Printed in Calendar of Wills, #1987.
