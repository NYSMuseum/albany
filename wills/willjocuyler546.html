Will of Johannes Cuyler - 1736



Johannes Cuyler

Wife Elsie - heir for her lifetime.

Son Cornelis - three pounds for his birthright.

Son Johannes, Jr. - share of clothing

Six daughters listed

House and lot on Pearl Street [east side in second ward]

Grounds

Also, lot on Pearl in second ward

Also Codicil dated September 27, 1738.



notes

Will filed or written on November 1, 1736. No probate date given in this document. Not printed in Calendar of Wills or Abstract of Wills. Printed in NYGBR 4:77-78.
