Will of Cornelis Gysbertsen Van Den Bergh - 1714


van den BERGH, Cornelis Gysbertsen, of Rensselaerswyck Manor. Wife Cornelia, children Gysbert, Gerrit, Tryntie, wife of Peter Waldron, Marritie, wife of Cornelis van Alstyn, Cornelia, wife of Martin van Alstyn, Mathyas, Geertie, Wynant and Goose.

Real property in Albany City and N. Y. City, homefarm on E. side of Hudson R., 1 mile back into the woods.

The wife sole executrix.

Witnesses Goose van Schaick, Anthony Coster and Volkert van Veghte.



notes

Will dated March 3, 1714. Probated July 6, 1717. Printed in Calendar of Wills, #1984 (p. 448). Abstract or actual document not yet found.
