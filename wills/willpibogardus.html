Will of Peter Bogardus - 1756


BOGARDUS, Peter, of Albany City, mariner.

Brothers Shiboleth and Jacob Bogardus, sisters Wyntie, Cornelia and Catherine.

Real and personal estate.

Executor bro. Jacob.

Witnesses Joseph Webb, Jacob de Garmo, shoemaker, and John de Garmo, yeoman




notes

 Will dated October 14, 1756. Probated . Printed in Calendar of Wills, #239.
