Will of Stephen Lush - 1825


LUSH, Stephen, of Albany City, esquire. Wife Lydia, daughters Rachel, Gertrude, widow of Robert James, Mary, wife of John M. Bradford, sons Samuel Stringer, William and Richard.

House and lot on N. Market Str. (Broadway) Albany, land on Sakendaga R., in Township No. 3 & 1 1 Old Military Patent, No. 27 Tot- ten and Crossfield Purchase, in Montgomery County, in lot No. 77 Jersey field, in lot No. 73 on Scaroon Lake, personal property.

Trustees for da. Mary Bradford's sons Samuel S. and William.

The wife sole executrix. Witnesses Richard S. Treat, Henry van den Bergh and C. Y. Lansing.


notes

 Will dated April 11, 1825. Probated October 16, 1826. Printed in Calendar of Wills, #1101.
