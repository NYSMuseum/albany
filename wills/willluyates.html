Will of Luickes Yates - 1766



YATES, Luickes, of Albany City, blacksmith. Wife Sarah, bro. Abraham I. Yates.

Homestead and personal property.

The wife sole executrix.

Witnesses Thomas Cooper, Obediah Cooper and Gerrit van Saute jun.

Albany Co. Records, Wills, I., p. 315.



notes

Will dated January 11, 1766. Probated February 28, 1766. Will summary printed in Calendar of Wills, item #2159.
