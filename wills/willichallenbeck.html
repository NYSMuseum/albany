Will of Isaac Casperse - 1708


CASPERSON, Isaac, of Albany City. Wife Dorithy, sons Jacob, Hendrick, Gerrit, daughters Marritie, wife of Wouter Vrooman, Elizabeth, Rachel and Hannah. Island in Hudson R. above Albany and other land, personal property.

The wife executrix. Witnesses Hendrick Hansen, Jan Rosie, Thos. Williamsen.



notes

 Will dated November 17, 1708. Probated March 28, 1728. Printed in Calendar of Wills, #255. Will abstract not yet found.

Probably refers to his neighbor Thomas Williams.
