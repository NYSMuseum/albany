Will of Edward Reims - 1702


REIMS, Edward, of Albany, victualler.

Wife Elisabeth

Daniel Wilkeson, John Taylor, drummer in Capt. James Weemes, Company.

Personal estate. Executor Lieut. Henry Holland.

Witnesses John Collins, John Oliver and John Carr.



notes

 Will dated September 14, 1702. Probated September 24, 1702. Printed in Calendar of Wills, p. 335.
