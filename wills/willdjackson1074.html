Will of Dinnah Jackson - 1818

Dinnah Jackson of the city of Albany

Recorded October 17, 1818

Granddaughter Dinnah, bed and bedding, one half of household furniture, clothing and monies.
        Also: Lots in 5th Ward #s 1, 2, 3, 4, 9 on Abm. Oake map of May 27, 1813

Granddaughter Jain, wife of John Williams, remaining half of furniture and clothing

Granddaughter Elizabeth $75; Granddau. Hannah $10, Grandson Harry $20

Also to Dinnah and Jain, lot #7 to share

Grandson John, lot #12

Will made June 29, 1818

Signed with mark

Executors: Christian Miller, Peter E. Elmendorph, Harmanus Wendell



notes

 Will "made" June 29, 1818; recorded October 17, 1818. Inventoried in Inventory of Wills, referenced to volume 5:96. This is a summary of the will contents as it was encountered during the 1990s when it was then residing at the Surrogate's court in the Albany County courthouse.
