Will of Jannettie Ryers - 1712

[Page 187.] In the name of God, Amen, May 8, 1712. I, Jannettie Ryers [Anna Jans Ryers Gerrits], of the city of Albany, widow of Garnett Ryerson, deceased.

I leave all my estate to my four children, Elbert, John, Maritte, wife of Hubert Van Dusen, and Ryer Geritts.

I make my sons Elbert and Ryer, executors.

Witnesses, Jan Lansing, Anthony Van Schaick, Thomas Willimse.

Proved at Albany, March 21, 1713, before Johanes Cruger.



notes

Will belonging to Anna Jans Ryers Gerrits dated May 6, 1712. Probated March 21, 1713. Printed in Abstracts of Wills, 26:104.
