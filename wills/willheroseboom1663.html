Will of Henry H. Roseboom - 1769


Henry H. Roseboom, Albany

Heirs:

mother Cathalina Maebie
sister: Susannah, wife of Isaac Truax
nephews: Isaac Truax, son of Isaac, Henry Truax
executor: brother-in-law Isaac Truax



notes

Will dated May 20, 1769. Recorded May 31, 1790. Letters of Administration granted August 14, 1790 to John Roseboom of Montgomery County. Printed in Abstracts of the of Wills, p. 117. The above is a simple calendar listing. We await a more substantive document!
