Will of John Van Vechten - 1762

In the name of God Amen. I, John Van Veghten, of Albany, “ now a major in the New York Forces.” I direct all debts to be paid. I leave to my wife Anattie all my estate, real and personal, to be at her disposal as she shall think proper, and I make her my executor.

Dated May 12, 1762. Witnesses, Gussie Van Schaick, Gerard De Peyster, John Visscher.



notes

Will dated May 12, 1762. Proved October 6, 1763. Printed in Abstract of Wills volume 30, p. 271. Online printing.
