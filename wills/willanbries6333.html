Will of Anthony Bries - 1702



B R I ES, Anthony, of Albany City, yeoman.

Wife Catharine, children Marritie, Nellitie, Hendrick, Catharina, Margrett and Eva.

Real and personal estate.

The wife sole executrix with her father Albert Ryckman and Anthony Rutgers as Tutors.

Witnesses Johannes Lydius, David Schuyler, Justice, and Rt. Livingston jun.

Albany Co. Records, Wills, I., p. 109.



notes

Will dated . Probated . Printed in Calendar of Wills, #245.
