Will of Johannes De Wandelaer - 1705

de WANDELAER, Johannes senior, of Albany, merchant. Children Anderis, Johannis, Sara, Catharina, Anna, Alida, Adriaen and Pieter.

House and lot in Queens Str., N. Y., land on the Plain, Albany, personal property.

Son Johannis and Thomas Williams guardians of minor children and executors. Witnesses Hend. Hansen, Justice. Johannis Mingael, Justice, and Evert Jansen.

"Albany Co. Records, Wills I., p. 142."



notes

Will dated June 20, 1705. Probate date not given. Calendar information only printed in Calendar of Wills, #567 (p. 132).

Transformed from an online resource by SB.

"The Plain": An imprecisely used term for Albany land a block or more above (west of) Pearl Street.

Probably this Evert Janse Wendell.
