Will of Abraham H. Wendell - 1793


WENDELL, Abraham H., of Albany City, merchant.

Sons Harmanus, John and Jacob, daughter Susannah or Sanneke, son of dec'd da. Annatie, vizt. John van Veghten.

House and lot in Albany, land back of the Fort on the hill, Albany, do. in Sacondaga Patent, pasture lot at Schonectady, personal property.

Executors the three sons and the daughter. Witnesses John Stevenson, Cornelius Wendell and John I. Pruyn.



notes

 Will April 13, 1793. Probated February 18, 1800. Printed in Calendar of Wills, #2104.
