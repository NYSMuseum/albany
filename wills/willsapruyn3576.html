Will of - Samuel Pruyn - 1752



PRUYN, Samuel, of Albany City, blacksmith.

Wife Maritie, sons Frans S., Johannis S. and Jacob S.

House and lot in 2d Ward, personal estate.

Executor Abraham Bogart of Albany City.

Witnesses John Ja. Lansingh, Phelip Muller and Richard Cartwright.



notes

Will March 27, 1752. Probated January 26, 1753. Printed in Calendar of Wills, #1381.
