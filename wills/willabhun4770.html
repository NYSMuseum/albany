Will of Abraham Hun - 1811


HUN, Abraham, of Albany City, counsellor-at-law.

Wife Maria, da. of Leonard Gansevoort junior, son 1814 Thomas, da. Elizabeth.

Houses and lots on Market and Dock Str. and on Union and Hannibal Str., Albany, personal property.

Executors the wife, father-in-law Leonard Gansevoort junior, brother-in-law Rev. Dr. John Basset and wife, and Rensselaer Westerlo.

Witnesses, Jno Evertsen. Abrm. A. Lansingh, and Isaac Hutton.




notes

 Will September 30, 1811. Probated February 20, 1812. Printed in Calendar of Wills, #905.
